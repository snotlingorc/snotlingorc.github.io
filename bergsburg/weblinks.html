Web Links

Here's a list of URLs and hyperlinks to people and organisations we believe will enrich your WFRP online experience.

  • Games Workshop

    WFRP's creators, their site offers miniatures, books and plenty of Warhammer flavour.

  • Warpstone

    The officially unofficial WFRP magazine.

  • Strike to Stun

    The online magazine for WFRP.

  • Liber Fanatica

    Fanmade v1 to v2 conversion utilities and other great articles.

  • Atoom's WFRP Resort

    A gathering of WFRP materials.

  • Oliver Rosenkranz's WFRP Page

    Lots of good stuff, especially on herbs, an out-of-date but still interesting FAQ, history, encyclopaedia and more.

  • Warhammer Forever

    Lots of stuff. Look for yourself!

  • Critical Hit

    Careers, religion, scenarios, world guide, bestiary and more.

  • The Imperial Explorer

    Great looking page.

  • Access Denied

    Need some players to send to Bergsburg? This is a huge player database.

  • Mad Alfred's page

    Author of the official sourcebook Dwarfs: Stone and Steel.

  • IGARoL

    Spanish language WFRP site. English version coming soon.

  • Thorr's Warhammer Maps Archive

    Lots of excellent maps. One of them even has Bergsburg on it.

  • The Shadow Warriors Roleplaying Club

    Go here and meet some members of the Bergsburg Council or order a copy of the excellent 'A Private War'.

  • Black Spectrum Productions

    Andreas Blicher's excellent maps.

If you want to be linked or you want your link rewritten then please contact us.
