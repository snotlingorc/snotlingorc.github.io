Bergsburg is now available in 2nd Edition

Bergsburg is an online project for and by players of Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay from all over the world. The aim is to develop the city of Bergsburg as a setting for inspired adventuring. If you want to know more, you should go to the Project Information page.

Just as likely, you are already dying to explore the intriguing city of Bergsburg, capital of Hochland and home of the legendary Temple of Shallya's Falls. In that case, you should start with an overview at the City Map.

Finally, you can navigate the wealth of material, including maps and a complete directory of Bergsburg NPCs, through the Bergsburg Indices.
