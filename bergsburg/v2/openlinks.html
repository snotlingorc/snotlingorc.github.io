Open Links

Looking for inspiration for a new submission? Check out all the loose ends and undeveloped threads dangling throughout the project.

Reading through some of the existing submissions can be inspirational for deciding on a new submission that would fit well into Bergsburg. Here is a list of some of the more obvious 'open links' and some references to them.

The district guides, reached from the City Map also include many short descriptions of people and places which are intended to be used as the basis for more complete submissions.

There is no such thing as 'The Thieves' Guild of Bergsburg', those references given are by those who wish to keep the name of one of the gangs secret, or who are ignorant of the ways of the underworld. There are in fact several gangs in Bergsburg, including The Kreuzers and The Hovenbachs (itself a loose coalition of minor gangs and smugglers). You are welcome to detail any of these.)
