Rumours

As any place in the Old World, Bergsburg is ripe with rumours of just about anything. From the seedy inns of Helmsberg, to the grandiose dinner halls of Harzel, everyone is eager to hear the news. As the saying goes, when you buy goods at the markets of Grossplatz, most vendors add a piece of good advice for free.

Here follows some of the latest rumours heard around Bergsburg. But beware, Bergsburg is a lively place, so chances are they have changed when you come back!
