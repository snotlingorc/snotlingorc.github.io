*Half Elves*


BY: James Del Pendley-JesterJD@AOL.COM

Half Elves are very rare hybrids that occur as a result of Elf's and Men mating. Anyone with Elf parentage(Sea, Wood, High) is considered Half Elf. On a whole Half Elves are a very small population in the Old World, although there are several small Half Elven settlements scattered nearby the Elven forest realms, and several neignborhoods in Marienburg. These communities exist as a stable race ( a Half Elf mating with a Half Elf produces a Half Elf ) and are grudgingly accepted by the neighboring human and Elf settlements. Half Elves are not totally accepted by Man or Elves, but can rise to considerable power and authority within either culture. There have always been rumours that many of the ruling houses of the empire ( including Sigmar himself )have Elven blood somewhere in their past.

Mandatory Skills Wood Elf Parentage: 

	All Half  Wood Elves have Excellent Vision. If the character has
        two or more initial skills, there is a 50% chance that the second will
        be one from Dance,  Musicianship or Sing with an equal chance of
        each. Any remaining skills should be diced randomly on the Skill
        chart for the chosen career class.
Sea Elf Parentage: 
        All Half Sea Elves have Orientation and Sailing.  If the character
        has two or more initial skills, there is a 50% chance that the second
        will be one from Excellent vision, Fish, Row, or Boat Building with
        an equal chance of each. Any remaining skills should be diced
        randomly on the skill chart for the chosen career class.
