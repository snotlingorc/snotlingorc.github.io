DUCK HUNTER

Advanced Career

Rich merchants and nobles with too much time and money on their hands, will often gather their friends together and go on excursions out to their town houses or country houses. After a considerable amount of drinking, they will get in boats and row out to the nearest swampy or marshy area. There they hide in the cold dawn, sitting in a marsh, drinking and waiting to shoot ducks. The novelty to their hunting is that they use expensive and rare firearms for their hunting. Mimicking duck calls, or flushing the ducks out with dogs, they will open fire blowing the harmless ducks out of the sky.

Afterwards, many will wander back to their hunting lodges for more drinking, and to treat those wounded by stray rounds...
