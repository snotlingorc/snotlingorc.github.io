Watch Commander has the responsibility to control the guarding of the streets in a city. He often is granted the tittle Marshal of .... He has a number of Captains under him and two other commanders beside him; the commander of the garrison and the army. Above him in the city is only the elector count or The Emperor. His job is to keep the streets and the city safe from inner troubles such as riots, thieves, troublemakers etc.

Garrison Commander has the responsibility to guard the city wall from the enemy. He is in control of defence of the city. Also he is often granted the tittle Marshal of .... He has a number of Captains undere him and two commanders beside him. Above him is only the elector count or The Emperor. His job is to hold the enemy out of the city, and keep thives, thugs and troublemakers of the wall.

Army Commander has the responsibility of the standing army. He is often granted the tittle General of .... He has a number of captains under him and two commanders beside him. Above is only the elector count or The Emperor. His job is to maintain the army and lead it when the elector count or The Emperor wishes to use it.

All the three commanders work together to keep the peace in the city.
