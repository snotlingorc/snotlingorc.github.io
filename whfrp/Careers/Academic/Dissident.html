These political careers are mostly based on 18th, 19th, and 20th Century political movements, and are probably not appropriate for everybody's campaign. The Imperial authorities are alot stronger in my campaign, so they come into play. They are useful careers in Bretonnia also...

POLITICAL DISSIDENT

Advanced Career

The Political Dissident is a member of a political faction who is actively sought out by the authorities as a threat to state power. This of course gives the political movements fuel for the fire, vindicating their belief in state persecution and oppression. The Political Dissident, once in hiding, will write a manifesto or book of some sort, which will be heralded as the next revolutionary spark. Many activists seek the fame and potential martyrdom of this career. Political Dissidents are famed for their resolve and feared for their rabid adherence to their dogma and doctrines.
